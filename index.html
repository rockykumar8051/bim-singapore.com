BIM PROJECT MANAGMENT

A COMPLETE RANGE OF BUILDING INFORMATION MODELING SERVICES

BIM-SG provide holistic support to project teams to ensure that our client’s digital deliverables are achieved throughout the project lifecycle. We work collaboratively with our clients to develop a strategy and provide clear, transparent guidance as to what is needed for successful project delivery. A typical project requires a combination of following methods.

  • BIM Modelling (Design to As-built stage)

  • Quantity Take-Off / Bill of Materials(BOQ)

  • BIM Coordination

  • Clash Detection & Issue Tracking

  • Technical Support

  • Coordinated COBie Delivery(Facility Management)
